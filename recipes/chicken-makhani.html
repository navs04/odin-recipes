Chicken Makhani

Description

Chicken Makhani, popularly known as Butter Chicken, is one of the most beloved dishes in Indian cuisine. This rich and creamy dish features tender, marinated chicken cooked in a luscious tomato-based gravy infused with aromatic spices, butter, and cream. The magic of Butter Chicken lies in its perfect balance of tangy tomatoes, earthy spices, and the silky texture of butter and cream, creating a melt-in-your-mouth experience. Traditionally, the chicken is first marinated in yogurt and spices, then grilled or pan-seared for a smoky depth before being simmered in a luxurious sauce. Served with naan, roti, or steamed rice, this dish is a comforting and indulgent treat that never fails to impress.

Ingredients

  • 300g boneless chicken, cut into cubes
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • ½ cup tomato purée
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp fresh cream
  • 1 tbsp yogurt (for marination)
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • ½ tsp red chili powder
  • ½ tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • ½ tsp kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Marinate the Chicken - Mix chicken with yogurt, turmeric, chili powder, and salt. Let it rest for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Cook the Chicken - Heat oil in a pan, cook the marinated chicken until golden brown, and set aside.
  3. Prepare the Gravy - In the same pan, melt butter, sauté onions until golden, then add ginger-garlic paste and tomato purée. Cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Combine & Simmer - Add cooked chicken, kasuri methi, garam masala, and cream. Let it simmer for 5-7 minutes.
  5. Garnish & Serve - Stir well, garnish with fresh cream, and serve hot with naan or rice!
